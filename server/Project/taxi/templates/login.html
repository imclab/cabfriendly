Welcome to CabFriendly!

With CabFriendly, you can save cash, help the environment, and meet new people simply by sharing a taxi ride accross town. You can even plan ahead and schedule rides for later. Your ride's path will be matched against our database to connect you with others nearby who are looking for a similar ride. All you have to do is meet up and share the fare!
